KINGSFORD MK1

A extension to an existing Californian bungalow located Kingsford. A reworking of an existing cottage into a modern family residence, with an open floor plan, prioritising natural light and seamless indoor outdoor flow to rear garden and outdoor entertaining area.
The existing brickwork was restored and extended to afford a new layout to the rear of the property, with internal plaster decals modernised and maintained where appropriate. The existing enclosed verandah was restored to the original intent, which serves as an asset to the street and private space for the owners to occupy all year round.
