Osbourne House

A contemporary transformation to an original cream brick bungalow sited on acreage in the heart of the southern highlands. Our approach defined an new entry and internal layout to the original bungalow that seamlessly integrates with its natural surroundings through expansive floor to ceiling windows and operable doors to garden courtyards.

The new glazing maximise daylight, offer panoramic views of the gardens and extends connection to outdoor entertaining. With the addition of a new master bedroom wing, replaced bedrooms with a second living room this country home on acreage has been afforded a new life.
