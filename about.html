Who We Are

Based in Manly on Sydney’s Northern Beaches, Pieter+Henry is a full-service architecture and interior design studio, specialising in sustainable, forward-thinking living spaces that celebrate the natural environment around them.

We’re with you every step of the way

Closely collaborating with you, our approach is to design living spaces that enhance and support your unique needs, dreams and life at home. We focus on using your property’s distinct positioning and environment to enable a sustainable design that harmoniously celebrates the natural world around it.

Creating beautiful living spaces that celebrate the natural environment

From concept to construction, new builds and renovations can throw up their fair share of challenges along the way. With years of experience in interior and exterior architecture, our approach is to make the process as easy as possible by offering support through every stage of your project. From ideation and design, to project management and approval admin, to interior design and styling at the very end, we help ensure that your dream home is true to its concept, completed on time and within budget.
